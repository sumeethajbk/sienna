About Us
Our Purpose
Cultivating happiness in daily life. At Sienna Senior Living, happiness doesn’t just happen. It’s what we do. It’s who we are. It’s what we get back from it. We’re all here to help create happiness.

It’s happiness that’s found in the little things we do every day. Real happiness.

Like contentment, satisfaction, pride. The kind of happiness that can come from feeling safe. From achieving a goal. From making play. The kind of happiness that happens between friends. The kind of happiness that happens in living rooms and kitchens.

Happiness like that is our purpose. It’s the reason we’re here. We cultivate happiness in daily life. And doing that everyday supports our vision to become Canada’s most trusted and most loved senior living provider.
